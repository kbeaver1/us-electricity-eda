# us-electricity-eda
This report was created as background research in applying for a Business Analyst internship with the Tucson Electric Power (TEP) company in 2024. To understand how daily spot prices affect TEP opeating margins, I gathered data from the U.S. Energy Information Administration from 2014-2024. Not much was discovered, except that prices are affected by changes in temperature and energy consumption, but I discovered an extreme outlier in the spot prices for June 2021, where a Power Purchase Agreement over the Palo Verde Peak hub resulted in short-term sales of $84 million dollars from TEP's affiliates.
