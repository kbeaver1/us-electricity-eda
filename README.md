# Market Research for the Electric Power Industry
This report was created as background research in applying for a Business Analyst internship with the Tucson Electric Power (TEP) company in 2024. To understand how daily commodity prices affect TEP opeating margins, I used R Studio to assess 10 years (2014 - 2024) of wholesale spot price data gathered data from the U.S. Energy Information Administration. Prices were favorable for TEP when there were drastic changes in temperature and energy consumption, or in the case of an extreme outlier, when a Power Power Agreement brought in short-term sales, such as the PPA that TEP signed with its affiliates for the Palo Verde Peak in June 2021, worth $84 million dollars (figure below).
