# Market Research for the Electric Industry
This report was created as background research in applying for a Business Analyst internship with the Tucson Electric Power (TEP) company in 2024. To understand how daily spot prices affect TEP opeating margins, I gathered data from the U.S. Energy Information Administration from 2014-2024. Not much was discovered except for the cyclical nature of spot prices, which are affected primarily by changes in temperature and energy consumption, however I did discover an extreme outlier in the spot prices for June 2021, where a Power Purchase Agreement over the Palo Verde Peak hub resulted in short-term sales of $84 million dollars from TEP's affiliates.
